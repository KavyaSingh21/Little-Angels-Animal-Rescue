About Me

Hello! I'm Sahil Karni, a passionate dog lover who couldn't bear seeing hungry dogs in my neighborhood. I started this mission with a simple goal: ensure no dog goes to sleep hungry.

This isn't a big operation - it's just me, my love for dogs, and the amazing support from kind people like you! Every day, I prepare fresh meals and personally deliver them to stray and shelter dogs in our community.

What started as feeding a few dogs near my home has now grown into a daily mission that reaches dogs across the city.

"Sahil's dedication to these street dogs is incredible. I've seen him out in all weather conditions, making sure these animals are fed and cared for. He's a true hero!" - Priya, Local Resident

My Mission

With your help, I personally prepare and distribute nutritious meals to stray and shelter dogs in our community. No fancy organization, no overhead costs - just direct help from my hands to hungry paws.

Every day, I follow a consistent route to ensure all dogs receive their meals. Many now recognize my scooter sound and come running when they hear me approaching!
