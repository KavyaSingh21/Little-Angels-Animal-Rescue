PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sahil's Dog Feeding Mission Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you visit our website or interact with our mission.

Who We Are:

  • Sahil's Dog Feeding Mission is a personal initiative run by Sahil Karni
  • We are not a registered organization or formal business entity
  • This is a grassroots effort to help feed stray dogs in our community
  • We operate with the highest respect for your privacy despite our informal nature

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information and only what's necessary for our mission:

Information You Provide Directly:

Type of Information When We Collect It Purpose
Name When you donate or contact us To thank you and send updates
Email Address When you donate or subscribe To send receipts and mission updates
Phone Number When you contact us (optional) To respond to your inquiries
Payment Information When you make a donation To process your donation securely
Messages When you contact us To respond and improve our mission

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Website Usage: Pages visited, time spent, clicks
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system
  • IP Address: For security and analytics
  • Cookies: To improve website functionality

We DO NOT collect: Social security numbers, detailed financial information (beyond payment processing), sensitive personal data, or information from children under 13.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to support our dog feeding mission:

Primary Uses:

Process donations securely and send receipts
Send updates about dogs we've helped with your donations
Respond to your questions and messages
Improve our website and user experience
Send occasional newsletters (you can unsubscribe anytime)

We DO NOT use your information to:

  • Sell or rent to third parties
  • Send spam or unwanted marketing
  • Share with other organizations for their marketing
  • Create detailed profiles for advertising
  • Track you across other websites

4. Information Sharing

We keep your information private and secure. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Payment Processors: PayPal, UPI providers (only payment data)
  • Email Service: To send you updates and receipts
  • Website Hosting: To keep our website running

Legal Requirements:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activities

We will NEVER: Sell your data, share for profit, give to marketers, or use for purposes unrelated to our dog feeding mission.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

Security Measures:

  • Secure Connections: HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Payment Security: We don't store payment card information
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal information
  • Regular Backups: Secure backup of important data
Important Note: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Website functionality and security Session only
Analytics Cookies Understanding website usage Up to 2 years
Preference Cookies Remember your settings Up to 1 year

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Your Rights Include:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe: Stop receiving emails anytime
  • Data Portability: Get your data in a readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, simply contact us at: sahil@dogfeeding.org

We'll respond within 30 days and won't charge any fees for reasonable requests.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary:

  • Donation Records: 7 years (for financial record-keeping)
  • Email Communications: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Contact Messages: 2 years or until resolved
  • Website Analytics: 2 years maximum
  • Account Information: Until you request deletion

CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

9. Refund Policy Overview

Our Commitment: While donations are generally final, we understand that mistakes happen and want to be fair to our supporters.

Since Sahil's Dog Feeding Mission operates on donations to help feed stray dogs, our refund policy balances fairness to donors with our mission's needs.

General Policy:

  • Donations are typically non-refundable as they are charitable contributions
  • However, we offer refunds in specific circumstances outlined below
  • All refund requests are reviewed personally by Sahil
  • We aim to be understanding and fair in all situations

10. Eligible Refunds

We will provide refunds in the following situations:

Automatic Refund Situations:

  • Technical Errors: Payment processed multiple times by mistake
  • Wrong Amount: You were charged more than intended
  • Payment Issues: Problems with payment processor
  • Unauthorized Charges: Someone used your payment method without permission

Case-by-Case Refund Situations:

Immediate Regret: Request within 24 hours due to financial hardship
Family Emergency: Unexpected financial crisis after donation
Duplicate Donations: Accidentally donated twice
Misunderstanding: Thought you were purchasing something instead of donating

Non-Refundable Situations:

  • Change of mind after 7 days
  • Disagreement with how funds are used (we're transparent about our mission)
  • Request made more than 30 days after donation
  • Funds already used for dog food purchases

11. Refund Process

If you believe you're eligible for a refund, here's how to request one:

Step-by-Step Refund Process:

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Email sahil@dogfeeding.org with "REFUND REQUEST" in the subject line
  2. Provide Details: Include your name, donation amount, date, and reason for refund
  3. Include Proof: Screenshots of payment confirmation, bank statements, or receipts
  4. Explain Circumstances: Help us understand your situation (we're very understanding!)
  5. Wait for Review: Sahil personally reviews each request within 2 business days
  6. Receive Decision: We'll email you our decision with explanation
  7. Get Your Refund: If approved, refund processed within 5-10 business days

Required Information for Refund Requests:

  • Full name used for donation
  • Email address used for donation
  • Date of donation
  • Exact amount donated
  • Last 4 digits of payment method
  • Detailed reason for refund request

12. Processing Times & Methods

Payment Method Refund Processing Time Notes
Credit/Debit Cards 3-5 business days May take up to 1 billing cycle to appear
PayPal 1-3 business days Fastest refund method
UPI/Bank Transfer 2-7 business days Depends on your bank
Other Methods 5-10 business days Case-by-case basis

Important Notes About Refunds:

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • We cannot refund to a different account or payment method
  • Processing fees (if any) may not be refundable
  • You'll receive email confirmation when refund is processed
  • Contact your bank if refund doesn't appear within expected timeframe
